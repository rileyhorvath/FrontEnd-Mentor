Our Stadium

Raymond James Stadium, located in Tampa, Florida, stands as an iconic symbol of the city's sports and entertainment landscape. Completed in 1998, this state-of-the-art facility has since become a focal point for sports enthusiasts and event-goers alike. Home to the National Football League's Tampa Bay Buccaneers, the stadium boasts a seating capacity of over 65,000, creating an electrifying atmosphere during game days. The distinctive feature of the stadium is the colossal pirate ship anchored in the north end zone, which fires its cannons to celebrate each Buccaneers touchdown, adding a unique and captivating element to the spectator experience. Beyond football, Raymond James Stadium has been a versatile venue, hosting major events such as Super Bowls, college football championships, and high-profile concerts. Its modern amenities, including spacious luxury suites, multiple HD video boards, and expansive concourses, contribute to an enhanced fan experience. The stadium is surrounded by lush landscaping and offers impressive views of the Tampa skyline, creating a picturesque setting for any event. Raymond James Stadium stands not only as a sports arena but also as a symbol of community pride, bringing people together to celebrate the thrill of competition and the joy of shared experiences in the vibrant city of Tampa.
